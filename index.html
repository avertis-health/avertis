The Challenge

Breast cancer represents a global health challenge, that affects millions globally, with early detection crucial for effective treatment and improved patient outcomes

    Nowadays healthcare systems are characterized by:

  • Missed detections
  • Resource-intensive processes
  • Long waits

The rising number of eligible women and fewer clinicians highlights the need for efficient and accurate screening processes.

The Solution

Avertis is an AI-based platform to improve patient care in breast cancer diagnosis by increasing accuracy and reducing delays.

Medical images are analyzed, length and subjectivity of traditional methods are eliminated. A comprehensive diagnostic process prioritizes the most urgent examinations.

  • Workload reduction
  • Improved accuracy
  • Prioritization of urgent cases
  • Personalized assesments
  • Tailored report generation

Healthcare providers can manage cases effectively, view examinations and integrate reports. Patients can access them with a clear explanation.

Redefining Breast Cancer Screening

Through automated image analysis and intelligent prioritization, Avertis streamlines the screening process.
